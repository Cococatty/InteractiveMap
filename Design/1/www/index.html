See what influences Citi Bike usage

May 27th, through December 22nd 2013.

This is a scatter plot of Citi Bike usage for every day from May 27th, through December 22nd 2013. Every point represents one day, and the y-axis is set to total miles travelled on that day. You can change the x-axis and how the points are classified with the dropdown boxes. More details on this analysis in this post.

Click on the legend to turn subsets on or off.
